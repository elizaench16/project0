About Gryffindor House

This network was established in 2010 by a pair of enterprising muggleborn sisters, Sally and Susie Hackmore. The Hackmore sisters created the site to allow for an easier way to keep in touch with classmates, make connections, and get updates on Hogwarts. Though the site had a limited user base at first due to limited internet access, with the creation of the World Wide Wizarding Web in 2015, this site has become the primary source of information for Hogwarts Alums.
