The Seven Habits of Highly Effective Slytherins

  1. Set a goal. Make a plan. If any part of your plan depends on divination, make a new plan.
  2. Aim for the legal, socially-acceptable route. This will make your illegal actions fewer and harder to detect
  3. If the law is not on your side, change the law.
  4. If you must break the law, don't get caught - Vanishing, Silencing, and Disillusion spells exist for a reason.
  5. Always have an alibi or a scapegoat. Invest in Polyjuice and Timeturners.
  6. "Slytherin" does not mean "cruel", it means "successful".
  7. Research the side effects of rituals BEFORE you use them.
